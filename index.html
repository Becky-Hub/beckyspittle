About

I'm dedicated to enhancing user experiences through thoughtful design and innovative technology. My background in extended reality (XR), user experience (UX), interaction design, accessibility, and sound has equipped me with a diverse skill set well-suited for the dynamic field of HCI research.

With a strong publication record and experience leading successful projects, I have proven my ability to produce high-quality, impactful work. In 2022, I received the prestigious Microsoft Research PhD Fellowship and later completed an internship with the Collaborative Intelligence team at MSR Cambridge. These contributions have been shared with wider groups at MSR, directly informing their research directions.

Fueled by the potential of XR and AI/ML to reshape how we communicate with digital environments, I’m committed to leveraging these technologies to create more practical, intuitive, and inclusive interactive experiences. With a broad and specialized knowledge base in technology, interaction, research, design, and development, I excel in applying logical thinking and methodical approaches to develop new concepts and solve real-world problems. I’m eager to utilize my technical skills and theoretical knowledge to produce high-quality research with real-world impact.
